Our Programs

Three pillars of service rooted in Max's values.

Max Soti Scholars Fund

Each spring, we award scholarships of $2,500 to graduating seniors at Plano High School who demonstrate a commitment to community service, academic perseverance, and personal integrity—qualities Max embodied throughout his life.

Program Highlights

  • 18 scholarships awarded since 2022
  • Open to all Plano High School seniors regardless of GPA
  • Selection based on a short essay, a letter of recommendation, and documented volunteer service
  • Past recipients have gone on to study nursing, education, agricultural science, and social work at Illinois colleges

How to Apply

Applications open each January and close March 31. Forms are available through the Plano High School guidance office or by emailing info@maxsotimemorialllc.us.

Community Outreach

Our outreach programs address everyday needs in Kendall County. Max believed that showing up for your neighbors—consistently and without being asked—was the foundation of a strong community.

Current Initiatives

  • Back-to-School Supply Drive (August): We distribute backpacks, notebooks, and school supplies to 200+ students each year through partnerships with Plano School District 88.
  • Holiday Meal Baskets (November–December): Volunteers assemble and deliver complete holiday meal kits to families identified by local social service agencies. In 2025, we provided 85 meal baskets.
  • Neighbor-to-Neighbor Home Repairs: A spring program where volunteer teams help elderly and disabled homeowners with minor exterior repairs, yard cleanup, and accessibility improvements.

Memorial Events

Each year, we host events that celebrate Max's life while raising funds for our programs. These gatherings have become a cherished tradition in the Plano community.

Annual Events

  • Max Soti Memorial 5K Run/Walk (September): Our flagship fundraiser. In 2025, over 320 participants raised $22,000. The route winds through Settler's Park and downtown Plano. All skill levels are welcome.
  • Spring Community Cookout (April): A free, family-friendly afternoon at Plano Community Park featuring food, lawn games, live acoustic music, and a "community kindness wall" where attendees share stories of generosity.
  • Scholarship Awards Ceremony (June): An evening event at Plano High School where scholarship recipients are recognized alongside their families, teachers, and community supporters.
