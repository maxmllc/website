Support Our Mission

Every contribution helps us carry Max's legacy forward.

Max Soti Memorial Inc is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 99-4586659). All donations are tax-deductible to the full extent permitted by law. We are committed to using every dollar responsibly—over 90% of funds raised go directly to program services.

Where Your Donation Goes

$25

Supporter

Covers a back-to-school supply pack for one student, including a backpack, notebooks, pens, and a calculator.

Most Popular
$100

Advocate

Funds a complete holiday meal basket for a family of four, delivered by our volunteer team during Thanksgiving or Christmas week.

$500

Champion

Sponsors materials for one Neighbor-to-Neighbor home repair project, helping an elderly or disabled homeowner stay safe and comfortable.

$2,500

Legacy Partner

Fully funds one Max Soti Scholar award, sending a deserving Plano High School senior to college with financial support and encouragement.

How to Donate

By Mail

Send a check payable to Max Soti Memorial Inc to:

29 Meyer Road
Plano, IL 60545

Please include your name and mailing address so we can send a tax receipt.

By Email

For electronic transfer options, corporate matching, or sponsorship inquiries, contact us at:

info@maxsotimemorialllc.us

We will respond within two business days with instructions.

In Person

Donations are accepted at any of our community events. Volunteers at the registration table can provide receipts on the spot.

Corporate & Matching Gifts

Many employers offer matching gift programs that can double or triple the impact of your contribution. Contact your HR department to find out if your company participates. We are happy to provide any documentation needed to process a matching gift.

Other Ways to Give

  • Planned Giving: Include Max Soti Memorial Inc in your estate plan or will. Contact us to discuss legacy giving options.
  • In-Kind Donations: We accept gently used school supplies, non-perishable food items, and building materials for our home repair program.
  • Fundraise for Us: Host a bake sale, lemonade stand, or birthday fundraiser on behalf of the foundation. We will provide materials and support.
