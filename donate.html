How to Donate

Online

Visit our Square store to make a secure online donation:

maxsotimemorial.square.site

Quick, secure, and you'll receive an automatic receipt for your records.

By Mail

Send a check payable to Max Soti Memorial Inc to:

29 Meyer Road
Plano, IL 60545

Please include your name and mailing address so we can send a tax receipt.

At Events

Donations are accepted in person at all memorial events and fundraisers. Look for the donation table at the #56 Memorial Ride and Summer Cookout.

Corporate & Matching Gifts

Many employers offer matching gift programs that can double or triple your contribution. Check with your HR department to see if your company participates. We're happy to provide any documentation needed to process a matching gift—email us at info@maxsotimemorialllc.us.

Other Ways to Give

  • Host a Fundraiser: Organize a bake sale, car wash, or social media fundraiser on behalf of the scholarship. We'll provide materials and support to help you get started.
  • In-Kind Donations: We accept donated goods for our raffle prizes and cookout events. Contact us to arrange a drop-off.
  • Spread the Word: Sometimes the most valuable gift is telling a friend. Share our mission and help us reach more supporters.

Transparency

We believe donors deserve to know exactly where their money goes. Max Soti Memorial Inc directs 100% of donated funds to scholarship awards. Operating costs, including event expenses, are covered through in-kind donations and volunteer labor. If you'd like a copy of our financial summary, email us anytime.
